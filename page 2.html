Importance of IT in business


There are many reasons IT is important in businesses but the main things IT adds to are advertisement through the numerous networks available to help your business grow, Increase performance levels, save money instead of paper documents, communication between employees and customers and also improvements within the company


Skills, Methods and resources


Resources are required to carry out project tasks, they can be people, equipment, facilities, funding etc. The skills you require mainly for a digital project would be leadership, scheduling, risk management, critical thinking and communication, these are skills that decide if a project will work with the people involved. To ensure everything planned in the project goes right you have to put methods in place to explain exactly how something will be done to ensure the correct outcome is reached
